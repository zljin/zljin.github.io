单词语法

Created2025-10-14|Updated2026-01-10|英语语法
|Post Views:

outline: 英语单词语法

动词：

谓语动词拥有时态和语气:
I eat rice.
I ate rice last week.
I will eat rice tomorrow.
I am eating rice.
I have eaten rice.

If I were boy,I would marry you.
If I should see the rabbit tomorrow,I would eat them.
I wish that I had eaten the rabbit.

系动词：be/keep/stay/seem/feel/become/turn/smell/prove 赋值的含义

助动词：辅助谓语动词做谓语
I have done it.
I don’t think so.
I can kill a wolf.
You should not smoke here.

非谓语动词和只做非谓语的句子成分，可以简化从句,记固定搭配
有不定时，动名词，现在分词，过去分词等四种形式

不定式
I decided to quit my job and travel.
Her words made me think deeply.

动名词
Smoking is harmful to health.
His job is teaching English.
a swimming pool.
I’m interested in learning English. 介词后加动名词是铁律

分词
要记：不规则动词表

The movie is boring, so I feel bored.
A sleeping baby, and a broken window.

当分词短语放名词后相当于一个定语从句，优势：简化定语从句，做状语
The man standing (= who is standing) there is my teacher.
The book written (= which was written) by him is popular.

独立主格做状语
通过 名词/代词+非谓语成分 构成的独立结构，本身不是句子，用来修饰主句，描述背景/原因/条件

当分词的逻辑主语不是句子主语时:
The manager being on leave,I will handle all urgent matters.
The deadline approaching, the team is working around the work.
The testing phase finished, we’re moving to deployment.

当分词的逻辑主语是句子主语时,不需要自带“主语”:
Hearing the good news, she jumped with joy.
Given more time,I could do it better.

代词

人称代词做主格/宾格：
I like you. You like him.He like her.She need them.they need money.

物主代词做属格:

形容词性物主代词 名词性物主代词
It’s my computer. The computer is mine.
our ours
your yours
his/her his/hers
their their

指示代词：this/these(指较近的事务) that/those(较远的事务)

不定代词
many/a few (修饰可数) much/a little(修饰不可数)
some(肯定句/一些) any(否定句/任意)

2者以上 3者以上
each other one another
either neither
each/both/other every/all/another

The carrot is bigger than the other one.
The rabbit has something important to do.

名词：分普通名词，专有名词，可数与不可数名词
普通名词:box->boxes man->men child->children
专有名词: Earth,Mike
不可数名词: water, air
可数名词:team,fruit,people,furniture(集合名词) carrot(个体名词)
名词所有格,属格: the rabbit’s carrot

冠词：
1、泛指：an hour/apple, a girl
2、特指：the(元音读zi) hour, the girl
3、零冠词：复数泛指/泛指概念/专有名词：girls are beautiful,Summer is hot,In Spring

连词：
He is not only a talented musician but also a great father.
Both my sister and I are teachers.
She likes neither coffee nor tea. She only drinks water.
You can choose either the blue one or the red one.

介词：to/of/in/for/on/with/at/but/by/from + 名词/代词 介词为前置词用来提供附加信息如时间/空间/方式/原因/目的等

形容词：做定语/表语/比较级和最高级，主要修饰名词
前置定语修饰名词: a new black table/the rabbit busy eating (形容词短语做定语)carrots
后置定语修饰不定代词：everything possible
表语表达主语性质：The boy is tall
比较级/最高级：the more…the more/is bigger than/as big as/the most beautiful

注意形容词比较级的变化
类型 比较级构成 最高级构成 例词
一般规则 加-er 加-est tall, taller, tallest
以e结尾 加-r 加-st large, larger, largest
重读闭音节 双写加-er 双写加-est big, bigger, biggest
辅音+y结尾 变y为i加-er 变y为i加-est happy, happier, happiest
多音节词 前加more 前加most beautiful, more beautiful, most beautiful
不规则变化 特殊形式 特殊形式 good/better/best, bad/worse/worst

副词：做状语，常以ly结尾，可修饰 动词如何进行how/形容词or其他副词更具体/修饰整个句子表达观点态度 （记一些固定用法）

方式副词：We need to communicate this change effectively to all stakeholders.
程度副词：I absolutely agree with you proposal. (extremely/relatively)
时间/地点副词：The rabbit ate some carrots indoors yesterday.
频率副词：we usually have a stand-up meeting every Monday.
疑问副词：when does the rabbit eat carrots? 用来构成特殊疑问句的词
连接副词：I don’t know why the rabbits eat carrots 引导名词性从句的副词
关系副词：This is the reason why the rabbits eat carrots. 引导形容词性从句的副词
句子副词：
Therefore/However/Furthermore/Hopefully/Frankly/Ideally/Actually/Obviously/Basically
