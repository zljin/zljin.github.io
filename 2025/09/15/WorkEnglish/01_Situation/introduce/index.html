introduce yourself in welcome meeting.

background:
Welcoming participants
First of all,I’d like to welcome everybody and thank you all for coming to today’s meeting.
Let’s go round the table and introduce ourselves.

answer:
I’ll be working on BE for our GenAI project.I’m excited to work with everyone.

introduce your company and production

1、introduce yourself:

My name is phil, I am an Software Engineer in ABC company.

2、reason for writing

I would like to talk to you about how our products has helped our clients to optimism process.

3、the value you can offer

reduce costs、minimise errors、optimise proceses、increase sales、improve performance.

4、Call to action and end.

I have attached additional information about this produce for you reference.
I look forward to hearing from you.
